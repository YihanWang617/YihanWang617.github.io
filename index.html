Biography

I am a incoming master student at UCLA, and got my B.S degree from Tsinghua University. My research interest is machine learning, especially to provide a theoretical analysis and guarantee for the robustness of machine learning algrithms. I am currently working with Prof. Cho-Jui Hsieh of UCLA on the robustness of machine learning models especially certified robustness. Before that, I have worked with Prof. Jie Tang on Recommendation Systems.
